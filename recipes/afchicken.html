Air Fryer Chicken Thighs

Description

These crispy Air Fryer Chicken Thighs have become a weeknight staple in my house. An easy recipe that you can whip up super fast!

Ingredients

  • 6 chicken thighs with bone and skin
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sweet paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried herbs such as herbs de provenance or dried oregano

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken with the juice of 1/2 of the lemon, then season with remaining spices on both sides.
  2. Rub the seasoning well all over the chicken then transfer to the air fryer, skin side down.
  3. Air fry 400F 12 minutes on each side, until golden and crispy and cooked through in the center.
